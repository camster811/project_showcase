My Story

I'm Cameron Stanford, a Computer Science student at Auburn University pursuing a Bachelor's degree with a Master's in Cybersecurity, complemented by certificates in cyber defense and in AI. My journey in technology began with curiosity about how systems work and has evolved into a passion for cybersecurity, reverse engineering, and full-stack development.

My work involves creating intuitive visualizations for complex binary analysis data using modern web technologies.

Beyond academics and internships, I'm an entrepreneur running Solis Strategies LLC, where I've developed a proprietary stock trading system with impressive results. When I'm not coding or analyzing markets, you'll find me practicing Jiu-Jitsu, powerlifting, or contributing to open-source projects that push the boundaries of cybersecurity and software development.
